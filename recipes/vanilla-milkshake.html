Vanilla Milkshake Recipe

A vanilla milkshake

This vanilla milkshake recipe is a short recipe that only takes 5 minutes and is made by Tania on Cooking for my Soul.

Ingredients

  • 4-5 cups high quality vanilla ice cream
  • 1-1.5 cups whole milk
  • Â½ teaspoon pure vanilla extract

Steps

  1. In a blender, combine the ice cream, milk and vanilla extract.
  2. Blend everything on medium speed. If you want your milkshake to be thicker, add more ice cream. Likewise, if too thick add more milk to taste. Serve immediately and enjoy!
